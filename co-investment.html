Co-Investment.

This scheme affords you the opportunity to Invest in real estate with your friends, family or partners. It involves a group of entities (Friends, cooperatives, investment clubs) pulling funds together for a real estate investment venture. As the capital is raised collectively, it is much easier for individual member to participate without breaking the bank.

The end game of the investment could be

      Invest to Own: Real Estate is an excellent store of value. You can also live in or earn via shortlets, rentals etc. while the value of the property appreciates.
      Invest to Sell: Completed projects can be sold for high returns
