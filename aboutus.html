WHO WE ARE

Regeneration is a full-service design, construction and development firm using our principles of value addition and sustainability to provide innovative and cutting-edge products and services on residential, institutional and commercial projects.

With professionalism, Regeneration strives to deliver outstanding quality from the project charter till post construction operations in view of meeting the project objectives as agreed with the client/customer. This ensures we develop trusting stakeholder relationships lasting much longer than the span of the project.

Being entrepreneurs, we pursue the achievement of technical objectives without compromising business objectives. Through thorough research and exemplary client/customer relationships, our process has been carefully nurtured over time using a dynamic, creative, progressive and efficient approach aimed at churning out cutting edge and innovative developments. This leads to satisfaction in form of project scope, time, cost and quality.

Vision

To be an international built environment leader/driver of high repute

Mission

Our mission is to plan and create spaces for people with a prudent use of resources while maintaining a high-level end product of desired aesthetics, function and value

Philosophy

Aesthetics

Use of responsive and expressive architectural language, character, elements and materials to fit and improve the vista of the urban scape.

Function

Creation of user-friendly spaces which promote good experiences in the form of vista, environmental quality, sustainability, thermal comfort etc. It is our purpose to provide effective and efficient spaces/buildings based on innovative design principles/fundamentals

Value

Increasing physical, social, environmental and economic value by providing spaces or buildings aimed at meeting client objectives and customer satisfaction as well as a healthy return on investment.

Value Proposition

On a project-by-project basis, we maximize the value of the built environment by infusing creative building and business solutions with necessary function and aesthetics through our creative design team, modern technology and practical knowledge of the industry
